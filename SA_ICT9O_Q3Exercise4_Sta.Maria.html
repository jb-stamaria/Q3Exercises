Tagaytay

On the Philippine island of Luzon, south of Manila, is the well-known tourist destination of Tagaytay. It is situated on a ridge above Taal Volcano Island, an active volcano encircled by Taal Lake, and is renowned for its temperate environment. With its location above the active crater of Taal Volcano, Tagaytay is guaranteed a spot on most travel itineraries to the Philippines. The town is a desirable vacation spot thanks to views of the crater lake and a cool, pleasant atmosphere.

What I did in Tagaytay: Went to Sky Ranch, Went to a Zoo, Sightseeing Near Taal Lake, Ziplining, Went to a Hot Spring, and Went to Famous Monuments.
